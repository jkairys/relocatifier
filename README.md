# relocatifier

It relocates you.

A personal research platform for deciding where to relocate a family along Australia's east coast (Cairns → Batemans Bay). Two modes: **Discovery** — choropleth maps and stat sheets comparing every suburb in the search zone (median age, prices, rents, yield, crime, climate, schools); then **Watchlist** — fresher, deeper data on shortlisted suburbs (recent sales, live listings, school proximity).

Successor to [school-map](https://github.com/jkairys/school-map), which is being mined for parts — notably its listings-scraper platform and MySchool NAPLAN scraper.
